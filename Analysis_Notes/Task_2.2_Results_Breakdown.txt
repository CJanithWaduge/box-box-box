4. TECHNICAL INTERPRETATION OF RESULTS
--------------------------------------
A significant observation is made regarding the small numerical values 
(-0.03s and -0.01s). Technically, this is interpreted as follows:

- The Impact of Tire Degradation: 
  The script calculates the 'Average Rank' across the entire duration 
  of a tire stint. While a SOFT tire is significantly faster at the 
  start, its rapid degradation (wear) slows it down toward the end. 
  The resulting small offset value is a byproduct of the initial speed 
  being balanced out by late-stint wear.

- Confirmation of Deterministic Patterns:
  The consistency of the results across 30,000 races confirms that the 
  simulation is not stochastic (random). It operates on a fixed 
  mathematical model: Lap Time = Base Time + Offset + Wear.
